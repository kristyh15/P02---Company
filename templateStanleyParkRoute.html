Stanley Park Route

Description:

Take a ride around Vancouver's largest and most beloved urban park. The park itself is sprawling with historic landmarks, scenic gardens, restaurants and Canada's largest aquarium. Starting at Canada Place, move along the waterfront till you reach the beautiful Stanley Park Seawall. The Seawall circles around the entire park and promises plenty of amazing sightseeing. If 10K sounds too long, speed things up by cutting through the various bike trails of the park and coast by the gardens to smell the roses. To end your ride, you will arrive at the gorgeous English Bay, where locals gather to bask in the sun and go for a dip.

Details:

  • Level: Beginner
  • Elevation: Low
  • Distance: 9.6km
