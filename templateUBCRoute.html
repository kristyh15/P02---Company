UBC Route

Description:

Bike along the beautiful sandy banks of UBC. The starting point is at Jericho Beach, where sailboats coast along the clear waters of English Bay. As you bike along the shoreline, you will come across Spanish Banks, where the soccer and volleyball enthusiasts gather on the beach. Tempted by all the snacks? Take a beack at a beach conession and enjoy the lovely views. Continue along the path and you will pass the University of British Columbia. Campus landmarks include the Museum of Anthropology, the UBC Botanical Garden, Greenheart TreeWalk and Nitobe Memorial Garden. End your bike ride at Pacific Spirit Regional Park, a lush forested areas reflecting the beauty of British Columbia.

Details:

  • Level: Beginner
  • Elevation: Low
  • Distance: 9.6km
