Suggested Routes Add to Cart





Better Grip. Better Control.

This soft leather grip makes your handlebar ever more stylish. Wrapped around steel handlebars and two layers of added padding, this grip gives riders great control, allowing them to have better posture.


Added Padding.

We listened to your feedback and added padding to this upgraded version of the Agatha bike. Filled with three inches of high density foam and shock absorbant springs, this saddle provides shock proof performance while relieving pressure.


Smaller Wheels. More Speed.

The wheels on the Agatha bike are smaller and thicker than all other models of the Spoke brand. We've made it so it's packed with power to be a good eight speed bicycle.


Bells. One Way to Accessorize.

Want to give a voice to your bike? It's our gift to you when you purchase two bikes or more. These bells add a personal touch and mounts onto handlebars of any Spoke bike model. Made from aluminum and coated with a layer of protection spray, this bell is resistant to rust.
